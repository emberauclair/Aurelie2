    fingerprints
    .
    .
    .
    you left your fingerprints
    all over my soul
    I can’t erase it
    I don’t want to
    you left your fingerprints
    deep in my heart

    You changed me, dear
    You seared my soul
    You cant
    take it back
    my heart will always remember
    your touch
    I will always remember
    the rush
    Its gone now though
    I wish for your touch

    -2021
